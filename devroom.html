Dev Room

The messy part is part of the design.

This room shows the process behind the project: logo meaning, character sketches, AI-assisted visual prototyping, and the design choices that shaped the game identity.

Final Logo Story

One logo, three sources of pressure.

The final logo is not just decoration. It compresses the whole game into one symbol: school, family, and choices all stacked above the player.

01Graduation Cap

Represents academic pressure, expectations, and the school system that frames the entire game.

02Cards

Represent decisions, branching outcomes, and the feeling that no option is completely safe.

03House Charm

Represents family support and family pressure — the place that should feel safe, but sometimes does not.

Character Process

Original sketches and character concepts were created by the team. AI-assisted coloring was used as a rapid prototyping tool to test the final visual direction, not to replace the design process.

Design Principle

“Lore should not sit beside mechanics. Lore should become mechanics.”

That is why Jade's public image, Kevin's recovery, and William's perfectionism are not just personality descriptions; they change how players make choices.
