Student Files

Three students. Three ways to break.

Characters are not classes. They are student mindsets. Their pressure becomes mechanics; their weaknesses become strategy.

OUTGOING STUDENT · SOCIAL ALL-ROUNDER

Jade

Everyone knows Jade. She seems balanced, likeable, and naturally good at everything, but her confidence is built on public approval. Criticism does not just hurt her score; it shakes the image she works so hard to protect.

AdvantageFriend-related events have a high chance of success.
DisadvantageSanity decreases faster when facing criticism.
Special TraitPublic Image affects the chance of success in social events.
Game RoleUse interpersonal resources, but protect sanity.
